# Bevy Open Siege Content Rating Notes

This document records the release-candidate content disclosures used for storefront rating questionnaires and platform review.

## Gameplay Content

- Genre: single-player lane-defense strategy.
- Theme: stylized greenhouse defense against fantasy undead creatures.
- Combat: plants fire projectiles, block lanes, slow, burn, freeze, or damage fantasy undead enemies.
- Visual intensity: non-realistic 2D/illustrated art, no blood, no gore, no dismemberment, no realistic injury detail.
- Audio intensity: synthesized music and stylized sound effects, no voice chat, no recorded speech, no jump-scare audio.
- Player harm: no depiction of harm to realistic people or animals.

## Sensitive Content

- No gambling, loot boxes, wagering, casino mechanics, or paid random rewards.
- No in-app purchases, premium currency, ads, subscriptions, or external storefront links.
- No online multiplayer, chat, user-generated content, account system, telemetry, analytics, cloud saves, leaderboards, or social sharing.
- No sexual content, nudity, explicit language, alcohol, tobacco, drugs, self-harm, or hate content.
- No real-world political, religious, medical, financial, or legal claims.

## Store Questionnaire Answers

- Violence: fantasy non-realistic combat only.
- Blood/gore: none.
- Fear/horror: mild fantasy undead theme only.
- User interaction: none.
- Data collection: none; local save data stays on the player's device.
- Purchases/ads: none.
- Suggested rating posture: suitable for broad audiences subject to storefront-specific review.

## Manual Review Required

Storefront rating systems differ by region. Before final submission, review the current platform questionnaire and attach the final answers to `QA_SIGNOFF.md`.
